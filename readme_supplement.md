### What This Work Is and Is Not

**What it is not:**
Opterium is not a replacement for the Standard Model in Lagrangian form.
It does not propose new particles, new forces, or new experimental setups.
It does not reject quantum mechanics, general relativity, or the mathematics
of Lie groups.

**What it is:**
Opterium is a derivation of the Standard Model's 19 free parameters from
a deeper structural layer — the layer that precedes the choice of Lagrangian.
The Standard Model tells you *how* particles interact given their properties.
Opterium asks *why* those properties have the values they do.

The relationship is analogous to the relationship between the periodic table
and atomic spectroscopy. Spectroscopy tells you exactly what light an element
emits. The periodic table tells you why elements have the structure that
produces that light. Opterium is the periodic table of the Standard Model's
parameters.

### On Peano Axioms: Not Rejected, Reassembled

A common misreading: Opterium "rejects" standard mathematics.
This is false. Opterium *reassembles* it.

The Peano axioms define zero as a formal convention: zero is whatever
satisfies a + 0 = a for all a. This is a behavioral rule, not a proof of
what zero *is*. The axiom works — but it works without answering the
ontological question.

Opterium replaces the postulate with a proof.

**Postulate (Peano):** "There exists a natural number 0."
**Proof (Opterium):** Zero is the unique state in the positive integer domain
with no multiplicative witnesses — R₀ ∩ ℕ₊² = ∅. This is not a rule we
impose. It is a geometric fact about the relational web. It is verifiable,
it is falsifiable (produce a positive integer pair whose product is zero),
and it is the only characterization that requires no prior agreement.

The distinction matters deeply. An axiom is accepted without verification.
A proof is accepted *because* of verification — and rejected if verification
fails. Every foundational statement in Opterium is of the second kind.

This is not a philosophical preference. It is a methodological requirement:
a framework that claims to derive physical constants must have no unverified
foundations, because any unverified foundation could be hiding the constants
it later "derives."

Opterium predicts 19 physical quantities. It uses one external input: the

This is a conscious architectural choice, not a limitation.

**Why m_e specifically:**

**The analogy with Standard Model conventions:**

### GLOSSARY

**Ternary minimum / Тернарный минимум**
The smallest relational structure that can sustain an irreversible, directed
cycle: three elements A→B→C→A. Binary systems (A↔B) have mirror symmetry
and cannot generate a preferred direction. Four or more elements decompose
or reduce to a triangle plus redundancy. Three is the unique minimum.

**Tick / Тик**
The elementary transition in the E8 temporal structure: inner triangle T
flips to outer triangle T⁻ (sign reversal of all three root vectors). The
tick period is 2 (T→T⁻→T). The arrow of time is the irreversibility of this
flip, forced by the antipodal asymmetry (dot=−8 vs dot=+4).

**Doctor / Доктор**
The closure verification engine. For any geometric claim, Doctor runs
multiple independent routes to the same address and counts failures (tension τ).
τ=0 means the claim is geometrically certified. τ>0 means at least one route
disagrees — the claim is fractured and the fracture pattern identifies the error.
Named by analogy with a doctor who diagnoses by checking multiple independent
symptoms, not a single test.

**Fano plane / Плоскость Фано**
The smallest projective plane: 7 points, 7 lines, 3 points per line, 3 lines
per point. In Opterium it arises automatically from the XOR structure of the
7 non-rest states of three-axis halving (GF(2)³). Every element {a,b,c}
satisfying a XOR b XOR c = 0 is a Fano line. The Fano plane is the
multiplication table of the imaginary octonions.

**Halving / Дробление (оператор H)**
The universe's primary operation: given a sum S, find the most symmetric
split (⌊S/2⌋, ⌈S/2⌉). For even S: exact split, D=0, no arrow. For odd S:
unavoidable asymmetry D=−1, zero loss in product. This D=−1 residue is the
arrow of time, the source of the Fibonacci sequence, and the seed of the
entire E8 structure.

**Closure geometry / Геометрия замыкания**
The proof method of Opterium. A result is proven when ≥3 independent geometric
routes arrive at the same address with τ=0. This is stronger than deduction
from axioms: if the axioms are wrong, deduction preserves the error;
independent routes with different dependencies cannot all fail the same way
unless the result is genuinely wrong.

**OPT_G / Оптимальный спуск**
The principle of descent toward minimum tension: among all witnesses of a
given product, the one closest to the diagonal (smallest |D|) is the stable
node. The universe "finds" stable configurations by arriving at the address
where maximum independent geometric routes agree — the same principle as

**Neutral shell / Нейтральная оболочка (126)**
For any E8 root r, the 126 other roots with dot product = 0. These roots do
not interact with r. The number 126 = C(9,4) is a combinatorial fact about
E8, not a choice. It appears in the Higgs formula as α⁻¹ − 126 = 11.036
(the "active" electromagnetic coupling count).

**Debt system / Система долга**
Exact representation of decimal numbers as (mantissa, debt) pairs:
value = mantissa × 10^debt. All operations are exact integer arithmetic on
the mantissa; the debt tracks the decimal cursor. Eliminates all floating-
point errors. Example: 0.3 + 0.4 = (3,−1)+(4,−1) = (7,−1) = 0.7, exactly.

**Zero-cloud / Нулевое облако**

The number 420 is ...000420.000... The zeros are not padding — they are the
medium. The decimal point is a cursor marking "where the ones column is."
Moving the cursor changes scale, not the excitation pattern.

**ΔT = 0**
The closure condition: tension equals zero. Used throughout as the shorthand
for "this is verified, all independent routes agree, the claim is certified."
When ΔT > 0, the fracture pattern tells what type of error occurred.

### On the Impossibility of Other Universes (A Precise Statement)

This framework does not claim that other universes are impossible in any
absolute sense. Such a claim would itself require a viewpoint outside all
possible universes — which is precisely what the Observer Immanence Theorem
(Part I, §I.0.4) proves to be logically unavailable.

What the framework does claim, precisely:

**Our logic defines our observable closure.** The ternary minimum (observer,
distinction, medium) is the minimal structure under which observation is
possible at all. Any structure that does not admit this minimum cannot contain
an observer capable of formalizing it. The formalization collapses before it
begins.

**A universe with different foundational logic would be inaccessible to our
formalization.** Not impossible — inaccessible. The difference is crucial.
"Impossible" would require an absolute standpoint. "Inaccessible" requires
only our standpoint, which is all we have and all we can have.

**The formal rules for projecting our logic onto a radically different
ontological structure do not exist.** Not because we haven't found them —
because the very concept of "projection" requires a shared structure between
source and target. If the target has no associative projection methodology
compatible with our observer's causal backbone, the projection is undefined,
not merely difficult.

This is not mysticism. It is the same statement as: "We cannot measure the
temperature of vacuum with a Celsius thermometer" — not because vacuum has
no temperature, but because the tool presupposes what the object does not
possess. Our logic presupposes distinguishability, ternary closure, and
immanent observation. A universe that does not possess these cannot be
formalized *from within* our logical framework. Whether it "exists" in some
other sense is a question our framework cannot address — and honestly
acknowledges that it cannot address.

**We are the observer. We are the reason.** The framework closes on itself:
the ternary minimum that grounds the mathematics is the same minimum that
grounds the possibility of having a framework at all. The universe could not
have produced a different observer capable of formalizing a fundamentally
different structure, because that observer would require the same ternary
minimum to be an observer at all.

This is not a claim of uniqueness of our universe. It is a claim of
uniqueness of our *position as observers within it* — and the resulting
uniqueness of what we can formally access.
